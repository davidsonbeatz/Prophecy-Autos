Frequently Asked Questions

What financing options do you offer?

We offer a variety of financing options including traditional auto loans, lease options, and specialized financing programs. Our finance experts work with multiple lenders to find the best rates and terms based on your credit history and financial situation.

How do I know if my car qualifies for trade-in?

Most vehicles qualify for our trade-in program regardless of age, make, model, or condition. The trade-in value will be determined based on current market conditions, the vehicle's condition, mileage, and service history. We provide free, no-obligation appraisals to determine your vehicle's value.

How long does the import process typically take?

The import process timeline varies depending on the vehicle's origin, shipping method, and customs processing. Generally, it can take anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks from order to delivery. We provide regular updates throughout the process and work to expedite whenever possible.

What maintenance services do you provide?

Our service center offers comprehensive maintenance services including oil changes, tire rotations, brake service, engine diagnostics, transmission service, air conditioning repair, and much more. We use state-of-the-art equipment and genuine parts to ensure the highest quality service for your vehicle.

Do you offer warranty on your vehicles?

Yes, we offer various warranty options depending on the vehicle. New vehicles typically come with the manufacturer's warranty, while pre-owned vehicles may come with a limited dealership warranty. We also offer extended warranty plans for additional peace of mind. Our team can explain all available warranty options for your specific vehicle.
