About Prophecy Autos

We are a premium car dealership dedicated to providing exceptional vehicles and outstanding customer service. With years of experience in the automotive industry, we've built a reputation for quality, integrity, and excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Prophecy Autos began with a simple vision: to create a car buying experience that puts customers first. What started as a small dealership with just five vehicles has grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive retailers.

Our founder, Michael Prophecy, believed that buying a car should be exciting, transparent, and hassle-free. This philosophy continues to guide everything we do today. We've assembled a team of passionate automotive experts who share this commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we've expanded our inventory, enhanced our services, and built lasting relationships with our customers. But one thing remains unchanged: our dedication to providing quality vehicles and exceptional service.

Our Mission & Values

At Prophecy Autos, we're driven by a set of core values that define who we are and how we operate. These principles guide our decisions and shape our interactions with customers, partners, and the community.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the vehicles we select to the service we provide. Our commitment to quality is unwavering.

Integrity
